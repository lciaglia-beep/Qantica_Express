System architecture and user journey

QSS in 3 levels

System architecture and user journey from registration to the narrative evolution of the audiovisual work.

Objective: User Registration, Evolution of a narrative work, and interaction with the QSS module to activate the Commercial phase, including the Marketing phase and Distribution phase.

Core Specifications

  • 6 protocols of the QIP method.
  • QSS Module Activation in 3 levels:
    • Level 1: Commercial → Games, Transmedia, Music.
    • Level 2: Segmentation & Social Media.
    • Level 3: Destination screen → Distribution and Auction.

Core Objectives

  • Improve the writer's creative process.
  • The writer lives a complete experience of their work's process.
  • Unite the creative and commercial aspects of the platform.
  • We will use this tool to generate conversations with writers.
1

Entry Registration

The user Logs in/Registers and submits their work. Their identity and track record are verified by automatically extracting their authorial information through API connections:

IMDb Filmaffinity Other associations
2

Initial Filter (Gateway)

Path A: Fails

The user receives a 1-page document with:

  • Detected issues.
  • Minimum recommendations.
  • Possibility to enter Test 2.
  • In the future, an improvement course will be offered.
⚠️ Restriction: Must wait 1 month before resubmitting the work.

Path B: Passes

The work meets the initial standards and proceeds to the next level of the main process.

Advances to Signature and KYC →
3

Signature and KYC

Identity verification (KYC) and legal formalization module upon entering the method. This step ensures the creator's identity and protects the work before starting deep and commercial processing.

4

Narrative Context Construction

Deepening phase. The system asks multiple questions to feed the technology and improve the accuracy of the initial analysis. Special emphasis is placed on building the character context and the following elements:

Characters Motivations Relationships Narrative World Genre Conflicts Tone Dramatic Arcs Themes
5

Analysis 1 & QSS Activation

Standard Output

Generation of the First Analysis through 20 criteria. A detailed report is generated so the author can improve and visualize the first IPIs (Indicators/Points of Improvement).

Commercial Connection (QSS Activation)

Level 1: Commercial Games, Transmedia, and Music.
Level 2: Audience Segmentation and Social Media.
Level 3: Destination Destination screen (Distribution) and Auction.
"Once the IP is approved, a game feasibility analysis is performed and an interactive experience based on the script is generated."
IMPORTANT: Game feasibility will only be performed once the work is approved by the method.
6

Improvement System (IPI)

"Improvement Issues" module where IPIs (Indicators/Points of Improvement) are marked:

Inbox issues
Critical Mandatory and blocking issues to resolve.
Warnings Important but non-blocking issues.
Minor Secondary details or optimizations.
If critical points are not corrected, the work will not pass the method or obtain validation.
7

Socratic Process

The author rewrites and evolves the work through a Socratic process, guided conversations, and precise dramaturgical analysis structured around:

Scenes

Pacing, visualization, and purpose in the story.

Dialogues

Unique voice, subtext, intentionality, and naturalness.

Characters

Transformation arcs and deep motivations.

Structure

Outline, plot twists, and dramatic progression.

Conflicts

Antagonistic forces, tension building, and stakes.

8

Second Analysis

Deep review and iteration of the changes applied during the Socratic phase. The system validates if the improvements have resolved the critical points and if the work is ready for certification.

9

Final Result

The entire evolution of the work is recorded in the Qantica ecosystem, enabling continuous learning and complete creative traceability.

The technological and structural area analyzes:

  • Creative journey.
  • Improvements made.
  • Author's decisions.
